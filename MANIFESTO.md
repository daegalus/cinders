# Cinders Manifesto

Cinders agrees with the spirit of the Forge Sparks license manifesto: software
is built from shared human knowledge, and people should be free to study, use,
change, share, and build on it.

For practical legal use, Cinders is distributed under the Blue Oak Model License
1.0.0. That license gives broad permission while preserving contributor
protections and clear notice requirements.

Cinders also preserves the original Forge Sparks MIT notice and license
manifesto as upstream context.
